◈ HOW TO PLAY

🏗️ Place TowersSelect a tower from the right panel, then click any grey tile on the map to place it. You can't build on the enemy path.
⚔️ WavesPress "Send Wave" to begin. Enemies travel the path to your base. Each wave gets harder with more enemies.
💰 EconomyStart with 150 gold. Earn gold by killing enemies. Use it to buy and upgrade towers.
🔧 UpgradesClick a placed tower to select it. Upgrade it for better damage/range, or sell it for 60% of its value.
❤️ LivesEvery enemy that reaches your base costs 1 life. Lose all lives = Game Over!
