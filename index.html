◈ Instructions

How to Play

Master resource management and strategic tower placement to hold the line across all 15 waves.

01
🗼

Place Towers

Select a tower from the right panel, then click any grey tile on the map. Towers cannot be placed on the enemy path.

02
🌊

Send Waves

Press "Send Wave" to start the next round. Enemies follow the green path toward your base. Each wave is harder than the last.

03
💰

Earn Gold

Destroy enemies to earn gold. Use it to place new towers or upgrade existing ones. Wave-clear bonuses reward efficiency.

04
🔧

Upgrade & Sell

Click a placed tower to view its stats. Upgrade up to Level 3 for increased damage, range, and fire rate — or sell for 60% back.

05
❤️

Guard Your Base

Every enemy that reaches the base costs 1 life. You start with 20 lives. Reach zero and the game is over.

06
⚡

Control Speed

Use the 1×, 2×, 3× speed controls in the side panel. Right-click the canvas to deselect your current tower type.
